The World Wide WEb (WWW), commonly known as the Web, is an information system enabling documets and other web resources to be accessed over the Internet.

The Web was originally conceived as a document management system. It was invented by Tim Berners-Lee at the European Organization for Nuclear Research, or CERN in 1989 and opened to the public in 1991.
